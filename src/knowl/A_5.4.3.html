Exercise 5.4.4

Evaluate each of the following indefinite integrals.

  1. \(\ds \int x^2 \sin(x) \, dx\)
  2. \(\ds \int t^3 \ln(t) \, dt\)
  3. \(\ds \int e^z \sin(z) \, dz\)
  4. \(\ds \int s^2 e^{3s} \, ds\)
  5. \(\ds \int t \arctan(t) \,dt\)
    (Hint: At a certain point in this problem, it is very helpful to note that \(\frac{t^2}{1+t^2} = 1 - \frac{1}{1+t^2}.\))
