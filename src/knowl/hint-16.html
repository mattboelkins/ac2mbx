Hint 1.3.2.4
  1. Show that the quadratic function has \(t\)-intercepts at \((2,0)\) and \((-1,0)\); \(s\)-intercept \((0,32)\); and vertex at the point where \(t = \frac{1}{2}\).
  2. Compute \(\frac{s(2)-s(1)}{2-1}\).
  3. Observe that \(s(1+h) - s(1) = (-16(1+h)^2 + 16(1+h) + 32) - (-16(1)^2 + 16(1) + 32) = -16 - 32h - 16h^2 + 16 + 16h + 32 - 32\) and combine like terms carefully.
  4. Consider the line through \((1,s(1))\) and \((2,s(2))\), as well as the line through \((1,s(1))\) with slope \(s'(1)\).
  5. Observe that whenever the ball is rising, it's position function is rising, and thus the slope of its tangent line at any such point will be positive.
