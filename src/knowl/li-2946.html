Item 3
Use what we know about geometric series to determine that values of \(r\) so that \(\sum a_k\) converges if \(\sqrt[n]{a_n} \to r\) as \(n \to \infty\). Solution

Since the terms in our series are all positive, we must have \(r > 0\). Now the geometric series with ratio \(r\) converges only for \(r \lt 1\),so the series

\[ \sum a_k \]

will converge if

\[ \lim_{n \to \infty} \sqrt[n]{a_n} = r \]

with \(0 \lt r \lt 1\).
