Item 3
To relate \(z\) and \(h\), the Pythagorean Theorem is natural to consider. By this famous result, \[ h^2 + 4000^2 = z^2. \] Differentiating both sides implicitly with respect to \(t\), it follows \[ 2h \frac{dh}{dt} = 2z \frac{dz}{dt}, \] and thus \(h \frac{dh}{dt} = z \frac{dz}{dt}.\)
