Item 2
The average rate of change on \([1,4]\) is \(\frac{f(4)-f(1)}{4-1} = \frac{-5 - 1}{3} = -2\). Similar calculations show the average rate of change on \([3,7]\) is also \(-2\). On \([5,5+h]\), observe that \[ \frac{f(5+h)-f(5)}{h} = \frac{3-2(5+h) - (3-10)}{h} = \frac{3 - 10 - 2h + 7}{h} = \frac{-2h}{h} = -2. \]
