By two applications of the chain rule (noting particularly that \(\sin^2(v) = (\sin(v))^2\), we have \begin{align*} m'(v) \amp = \amp \frac{1}{\sin^2(v)+1} \cdot \frac{d}{dv} \left[ \sin^2(v) + 1 \right]\\ \amp = \amp \frac{1}{\sin^2(v)+1} \cdot \left[ 2\sin(v)\cos(v) \right] \end{align*}
