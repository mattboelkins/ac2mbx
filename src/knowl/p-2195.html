This series is not a geometric series, but this activity will illustrate how we might compare this series to a geometric one. Recall that a series \(\sum a_k\) is geometric if the ratio \(\frac{a_{k+1}}{a_k}\) is always the same. For the series in (8.3.4), note that \(a_k = \frac{2^k}{3^k-k}\).
