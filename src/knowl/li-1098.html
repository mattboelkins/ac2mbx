Item 3
To find an equation of the line tangent to the graph of \(x = y^5 - 5y^3 + 4y\) at the point \((0, 1)\), we only need the slope of the tangent line. Hence we compute \[ \left. \frac{dy}{dx} \right|_{(0,1)} = \frac{1}{5 \cdot 1^4 - 15 \cdot 1^2 + 4} = -\frac{1}{6}. \] Therefore, the equation of the tangent line is \[ y - 1 = -\frac{1}{6}(x-0) \] or \(y = -\frac{1}{6}x + 1\).
