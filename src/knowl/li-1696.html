For this Riemann sum with five subintervals, \(\triangle t = \frac{5-1}{5} = \frac{4}{5}\), so \(t_0 = 1\), \(t_1 = 1.8\), \(t_2 = 2.6\), \(t_3 = 3.4\), \(t_4 = 4.2\) and \(t_5 = 4\). It follows that \begin{align*} M_5 \amp = \amp v(1.4) \triangle t + v(2.2) \triangle t + v(3.0) \triangle t + v(3.8) \triangle t + v(4.6)\\ \amp = \amp \frac{7}{25} \cdot \frac{4}{5} - \frac{17}{25} \cdot \frac{4}{5} - 1 \cdot \frac{4}{5} - \frac{17}{25} \cdot \frac{4}{5} + \frac{7}{25} \cdot \frac{4}{5}\\ \amp = \amp -\frac{36}{25} = -1.44 \end{align*} A sketch is shown below.
