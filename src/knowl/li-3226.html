Item 4
Now show that \begin{equation} \sum_{k=0}^{\infty} a_kx^{k+1} = \sum_{k=1}^{\infty} a_{k-1}x^k. \label{eq_PA8.6_Airy_sum_2}\tag{8.6.7}\end{equation}Solution

As we did in the previous part,

\begin{align*} \sum_{k=0}^{\infty} a_kx_{k+1} \amp = a_0x + a_1x^2 + a_2x^3 + \cdots + a_kx^{k+1} + \cdots \notag\\ \amp = a_0x + a_1x^2 + a_2x^3 + \cdots + a_{k-1}x^{k} + \cdots \notag\\ \amp = \sum_{k=1}^{\infty} a_{k-1}x^k. \end{align*}
