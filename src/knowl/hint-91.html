Hint 2.8.3.1
  1. Remember that \(\ln(x) \to \infty\) as \(x \to infty\).
  2. Both the numerator and denominator tend to \(\infty\) as \(x \to \infty\).
  3. Note that \(x \to 0^+\), not \(\infty\).
  4. As \(x \to \frac{\pi}{2}^-\), \(\tan(x) \to \infty\).
  5. Observe that \(e^{-x} = \frac{1}{e^x}\).
