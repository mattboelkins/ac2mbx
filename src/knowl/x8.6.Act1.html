Exercise 8.6.5

Determine the interval of convergence of each power series.

  1. \(\ds \sum_{k=1}^{\infty} \frac{(x-1)^k}{3k}\)
  2. \(\ds \sum_{k=1}^{\infty} kx^k\)
  3. \(\ds \sum_{k=1}^{\infty} \frac{k^2(x+1)^k}{4^k}\)
  4. \(\ds \sum_{k=1}^{\infty} \frac{x^k}{(2k)!}\)
  5. \(\ds \sum_{k=1}^{\infty} k!x^k\)
