Item 3
The average of \(L_4\) and \(R_4\) is \[ \frac{L_4 + M_4}{2} = \frac{311+335}{96} = \frac{646}{96} \ne \frac{637}{96} = M_4. \] This average actually measures what would result from using four trapezoids, rather than rectangles, to estimate the area on each subinterval. One reason this is so is because the area of a trapezoid is the average of the bases times the width, and the “bases” are given by the function values at the left and right endpoints.
