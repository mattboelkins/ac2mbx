Hint 1.5.3.6
  1. Remember that \(f'(a) \approx \frac{f(a+h)-f(a-h)}{2h}\) and use appropriate values of \(a\) and \(h\).
  2. Complete the following sentence: “When the car is traveling at 80 kilometers per hour, it is using fuel at a rate of 0.015 \(\ldots\)”
  3. Remember that units on the derivative are “units of output per unit of input,” even when the input and/or output are rates themselves.
