Solution 3.1

Note that

\begin{align*} \sum_{k=2}^{\infty} (k-1)ka_kx_{k-2} \amp = (1)(2)a_2 + (2)(3)a_3x + (3)(4)a_4x^2 + (4)(5)a_5x^3 + \cdots + (k-1)(k)a_{k+2}x^{k-2} + \cdots\\ \amp = (0+1)(0+2)x^0 + (1+1)(1+2)x^1 + (2+1)(2+2)x^2 + \cdots + (k+1)(k+2)x^k + \cdots. \end{align*}

In other words, we can re-index this series by increasing every \(k\) by 2, or replacing \(k-2\) with \(k\), \(k-1\) with \(k+1\) and \(k\) with \(k+2\). This gives us equation (8.6.6).
