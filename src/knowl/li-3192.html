Item 4
We use the Ratio Test with \(a_k = \frac{|x|^k}{(2k)!}\): \begin{align*} \lim_{k \to \infty} \frac{ \frac{|x|^{k+1}}{(2(k+1))!} }{ \frac{|x|^k}{(2k)!} } \amp = \lim_{k \to \infty} |x|\frac{(2k)!}{(2(k+1))!}\\ \amp = |x| \lim_{k \to \infty} \frac{1}{(2k+2)(2k+1)}\\ \amp = 0. \end{align*} So the power series \(\ds \sum_{k=1}^{\infty} \frac{x^k}{(2k)!}\) converges absolutely on the interval \((-\infty, \infty)\).
