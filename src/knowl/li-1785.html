Item 4
We know \(v_{\mbox{\tiny{AVG}} }[a,b] = \frac{1}{b-a} \int_a^b v(t) \, dt\), so \[ v_{\mbox{\tiny{AVG}} }[0,4] = \frac{1}{4-0} \int_0^4 \sqrt{4-(t-2)^2} \, dt = \frac{1}{4} 2\pi = \frac{\pi}{2}, \] which is measured in meters per minute, since the units on “4” are minutes and on “\(2\pi\)” are meters.
