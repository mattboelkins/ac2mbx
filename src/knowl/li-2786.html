Item 2
When we subtract \(rS_n\) from \(S_n\) the middle terms all cancel and we are left with \begin{align*} S_n - rS_n \amp = \left(a+ar+ar^2 + \cdots + ar^{n-1}\right) - \left(ar+ar^2+ar^3 + \cdots + ar^n\right)\\ \amp =a + (ar-ar) + \left(ar^2-ar^2\right) + \cdots + \left(ar^{n-1}-ar^{n-1}\right) - ar^n\\ \amp = a - ar^n. \end{align*}
