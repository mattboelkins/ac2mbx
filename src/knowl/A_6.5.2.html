Exercise 6.5.3

Determine whether each of the following improper integrals converges or diverges. For each integral that converges, find its exact value.

  1. \(\ds \int_1^{\infty} \frac{1}{x^2} \, dx\)
  2. \(\ds \int_0^{\infty} e^{-x/4} \, dx\)
  3. \(\ds \int_2^{\infty} \frac{9}{(x+5)^{2/3}} \, dx\)
  4. \(\ds \int_4^{\infty} \frac{3}{(x+2)^{5/4}} \, dx\)
  5. \(\ds \int_0^{\infty} x e^{-x/4} \, dx\)
  6. \(\ds \int_1^{\infty} \frac{1}{x^p} \, dx\), where \(p\) is a positive real number
