Item 9
This series is an alternating series of the form \(\ds \sum_{k=1}^{\infty} (-1)^{k+1} a_k\) with \(a_k = \frac{1}{\sqrt{k+1}}\). Since \[ \ds \lim_{k \to \infty} \frac{1}{\sqrt{k+1}} = 0 \] and \(\frac{1}{\sqrt{k+1}}\) decreases to 0, the Alternating Series Test shows that \(\ds \sum_{k=1}^{\infty} \ \frac{(-1)^{k+1}}{\sqrt{k+1}}\) converges.
