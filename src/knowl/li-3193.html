Item 5
We use the Ratio Test with \(a_k = k!|x|^k\): \begin{align*} \lim_{k \to \infty} \frac{ (k+1)!|x|^{k+1} }{ k!|x|^k} \amp = \lim_{k \to \infty} |x|(k+1)\\ \amp = \infty \end{align*} unless \(x=0\). So the interval of convergence of the power series \(\ds \sum_{k=1}^{\infty} \frac{x^k}{k!}\) is \(\{0\}\).
