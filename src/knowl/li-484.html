Item 3
Following up on our work in (b), note that whenever \(h > 0\), \(|h| = h\), and thus \[ \lim_{h \to 0^+} \frac{|h|}{h} = \lim_{h \to 0^+} \frac{h}{h} = 1, \] while whenever \(h \lt 0\), \(|h| = -h\), and thus \[ \lim_{h \to 0^-} \frac{|h|}{h} = \lim_{h \to 0^-} \frac{-h}{h} = -1 \] Since the right- and left-hand limits are not equal, it follows that \[ g'(0) = \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{|h|}{h} \] does not exist.
