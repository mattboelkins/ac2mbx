Hint 1.4.2.2Points where the slope of the tangent line is equal to zero are particularly important. Try finding these points first in your effort to plot \(y = f'(x)\) and plotting those zero values on the axes where you'll graph \(y = f'(x)\). After doing so, think carefully as well about the questions: at this point, is \(f'(x)\) positive or negative? is \(f'(x)\) big or small.
