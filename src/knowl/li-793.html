Item 3
We first compute \(R'(x)\). By the quotient rule, \[ R'(x) = \frac{(x^2 - 9)(2x - 2) - (x^2 - 2x - 8)(2x)}{(x^2 - 9)^2}. \] From this, it follows that \(R'(0) = \frac{(-9)(-2)-(-8)(0)}{(-9)^2} = -\frac{2}{9}\), which is the slope of the tangent line to the curve at the point where \(x = 0\).
