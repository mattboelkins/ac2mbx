Item 2
Volume is the area of the base times the height, so \(V = \pi r^2 h\). Surface area is the area of the lids plus the area of the side, the latter of which is a rectangle with height \(h\) and width the perimeter of the base. Hence, \(S = 2 \pi r^2 + 2 \pi r h\). Finally, the total cost is the cost of the lids plus the cost of the sides, which is \[ C = 2 \pi r^2 \cdot 0.027 + 2 \pi r h \cdot 0.015. \]
