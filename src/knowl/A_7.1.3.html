Exercise 7.1.3

Consider the differential equation

\[ \frac{dv}{dt} = 1.5 - 0.5v. \]

Which of the following functions are solutions of this differential equation?

  1. \(v(t) = 1.5t - 0.25t^2\).
  2. \(v(t) = 3 + 2e^{-0.5t}\).
  3. \(v(t) = 3\).
  4. \(v(t) = 3 + Ce^{-0.5t}\) where \(C\) is any constant.
