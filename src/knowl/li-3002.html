Item 1
Each term in the series \[ 1 - \frac{1}{4} - \frac{1}{9} + \frac{1}{16} + \frac{1}{25} + \frac{1}{36} - \frac{1}{49} - \frac{1}{64} - \frac{1}{81} - \frac{1}{100} + \cdots \] is of the form \(\frac{1}{k^2}\) or \(-\frac{1}{k^2}\). Since each of these terms is less than or equal to \(\frac{1}{k^2}\) it follows that \[ 1 - \frac{1}{4} - \frac{1}{9} + \frac{1}{16} + \frac{1}{25} + \frac{1}{36} - \frac{1}{49} - \frac{1}{64} - \frac{1}{81} - \frac{1}{100} + \cdots \lt \sum_{k=1}^{\infty} \frac{1}{k^2}. \]
