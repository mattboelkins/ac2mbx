Item 1
Show that \(\frac{S_n+S_{n+1}}{2} = S_n + \frac{1}{2}(-1)^{n+2} a_{n+1}\). Solution

Since \(S_{n+1} = S_n + (-1)^{n+2}a_{n+1}\) we have

\begin{align*} \frac{S_n+S_{n+1}}{2} \amp = \frac{S_n+S_{n} + (-1)^{n+2}a_{n+1}}{2}\\ \amp = \frac{2S_{n} + (-1)^{n+2}a_{n+1}}{2}\\ \amp = S_n + \frac{1}{2}(-1)^{n+2}a_{n+1}. \end{align*}
