Item 3
Finally, we consider the most general case. Let \(a\) and \(r\) be real numbers with \(-1\lt r\lt 1\), let \(n\) be a positive integer, and consider the sum \[ \sum_{k=n}^{\infty} ar^k = ar^n+ar^{n+1}+ar^{n+2} + \cdots . \] Remove the common factor of \(ar^n\) from each term to find the sum of the series.
