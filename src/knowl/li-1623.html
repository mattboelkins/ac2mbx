Item 3
By calculating the area bounded by the curve, we find 1 unit of area on \([0,1]\), 4 units of area on \([1,4]\), and 8 units of area on \([4,8]\), thus the total distance traveled on \(0 \le t \le 8\) is \(D = 1 + 4 + 8\) meters. As the change in position is given by the net signed area on this interval, we find that the change in position is \[ s(8) - s(0) = 1 - 4 + 8 = 5 \ \mbox{m} . \]
