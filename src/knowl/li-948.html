Item 3
By the quotient rule, \[ h'(y) = \frac{(e^{4y}+1) \frac{d}{dy}[\cos(10y)] - \cos(10y) \frac{d}{dy}[e^{4y}+1]}{(e^{4y}+1)^2}. \] Applying the chain rule to differentiate \(\cos(10y)\) and \(e^{4y}\), it follows \[ h'(y) = \frac{(e^{4y}+1) [-10\sin(10y)] - \cos(10y) [4e^{4y}]}{(e^{4y}+1)^2}. \]
