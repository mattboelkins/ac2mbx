Item 1
Assume \[ \sqrt[n]{a_n} \to r \] as \(n\) goes to infinity. Explain why this tells us that \(a_n \approx r^n\) for large values of \(n\). Solution

If \(\sqrt[n]{a_n} \to r\), then as \(n\) increases we know that \(\sqrt[n]{a_n} \approx r\). So for large \(n\) we have \(a_n \approx r^n\).
