Using the limit definition of the derivative, we find that \begin{align*} f'(1) \amp = \amp \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{f(1+h) - f(1)}{h}\\ \amp = \amp \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{(3 - 2(1+h)) - (3-2)}{h}\\ \amp = \amp \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{3 - 2 - 2h - 1}{h}\\ \amp = \amp \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{-2h}{h}\\ \amp = \amp \lim_{h \to 0} -2\\ \amp = \amp -2. \end{align*}
