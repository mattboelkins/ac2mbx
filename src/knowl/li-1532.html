Item 2
To relate \(\theta\) and \(h\), observe that the tangent function is a good choice, since \(\tan(\theta) = \frac{h}{4000}\), so that \[ h = 4000 \tan(\theta). \] Differentiating implicitly with respect to \(t\), we find that \[ \frac{dh}{dt} = 4000 \sec^2 (\theta) \frac{d\theta}{dt}. \]
