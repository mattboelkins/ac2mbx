Hint 2.8.2.4
  1. Don't forget that \(f'(a)\) measures the slope of the tangent line to \(y = f(x)\) at the point \((a,f(a))\). Think about whether or not L'Hopital's Rule applies to the limit under consideration.
  2. Do the functions \(p\) and \(q\) meet the criteria of L'Hopital's Rule? If not, what are your options for evaluating the limit?
  3. Remember that L'Hopital's Rule can be applied more than once to a particular limit and that the sign of \(f''(a)\) is connected to to the concavity of the graph of \(f\) at the value \(x = a\).
