\item Consider the functions \(r(t) = t^t\) and \(s(t) = \arccos(t)\), for which you are given the facts that \(r'(t) = t^t(\ln(t) + 1)\) and \(s'(t) = -\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-t^2}}\). Do not be concerned with where these derivative formulas come from. We restrict our interest in both functions to the domain \(0 \lt t \lt 1\).
