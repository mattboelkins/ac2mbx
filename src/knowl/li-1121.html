Item 1
Differentiating with respect to \(x\), \[ \frac{d}{dx}[x^3 - y^3] = \frac{d}{dx}[6xy], \] so that by the chain and product rules we have \[ 3x^2 - 3y^2 \frac{dy}{dx} = 6x\frac{dy}{dx}+ 6y. \] Rearranging to get all terms with \(\frac{dy}{dx}\) on the same side, it follows that \[ -3y^2 \frac{dy}{dx} - 6x\frac{dy}{dx} = 6y-3x^2, \] and thus \[ \frac{dy}{dx}(-3y^2 - 6x) = 6y-3x^2. \] Finally, we have established that \[ \frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{6y-3x^2}{-3y^2 - 6x}, \] so evaluating at \((-3,3)\), we have \(\left. \frac{dy}{dx} \right|_{(-3,3)} = \frac{6(3)-3(-3)^2}{-3(3)^2 - 6(3)} = -1.\) Thus, the tangent line has equation \(y - 3 = -1(x+3).\)
