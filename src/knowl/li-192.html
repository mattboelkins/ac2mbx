Item 4
Using \(h = 0.00001\), we find \(\frac{P(2+0.00001)-P(2)}{0.00001} \approx 7457\); using \(h = -0.00001\), we find \(\frac{P(2-0.00001)-P(2)}{-0.00001} \approx 7460\). Averaging these two results, we find that \[ P'(2) = \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{P(2+h)-P(2)}{h} \approx 7458.5 \] which is measured in people per decade.
