Item 1
Consider the sum \[ \sum_{k=1}^{\infty} (2)\left(\frac{1}{3}\right)^k = (2)\left(\frac{1}{3}\right) + (2)\left(\frac{1}{3}\right)^2 + (2)\left(\frac{1}{3}\right)^3 + \cdots . \] Remove the common factor of \((2)\left(\frac{1}{3}\right)\) from each term and hence find the sum of the series.
