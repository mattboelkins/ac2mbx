Using the sum and constant multiple rules, it follows first that \[ g'(z) = 3 \frac{d}{dz}[z^7 e^z] - 2\frac{d}{dz}[z^2 \sin(z)] + \frac{d}{dz}\left[ \frac{z}{z^2 + 1} \right]. \] Applying the product rule in the first two terms and the quotient rule in the third, we find that \[ g'(z) = 3 [z^7 e^z + 7z^6e^z] - 2[z^2 \cos(z) + 2z\sin(z)] + \frac{(z^2+1) 1 - z(2z)}{(z^2 + 1)^2}. \]
