Item 3
Use your result from (b) to decide whether the sequence \(\left\{\frac{b^n}{n!}\right\}\) converges or diverges. If the sequence \(\left\{\frac{b^n}{n!}\right\}\) converges, to what does it converge? Explain your reasoning. Solution

\solution Since the series \(\sum \frac{b^n}{n!}\) converges, the Divergence Test tells us that the sequence \(\left\{\frac{b^n}{n!}\right\}\) has to converge to 0.
